Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site will be named "LocalBizConnect Chamber of Commerce." This name reflects the local nature of our chamber and our aim to connect businesses within our community.

Site Purpose

The website serves two main purposes:

  • Provide a platform for local businesses to promote their products and services.
  • Offer information about our community, local events, and resources to visitors and potential investors.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
  • How can local businesses become chamber members?

Color Schema

We have chosen a color palette that reflects our community's spirit and professionalism:

  • Background: #003459
  • Heading Text: #fff
  • Content Text: #000

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Headings: "Arial", sans-serif
  • Body Text: "Arial", sans-serif
